About

Welcome to Veterinarian Management System (VMS), your comprehensive solution for managing animal medical records online. Our system allows veterinarians, and pet owners to easily access and download animal reports from anywhere, ensuring timely and consistent care. With VMS, stay informed and actively participate in your pet’s healthcare decisions, while securely backing up essential medical information.
